About Us

Welcome to Express Eat

CraveCraft, formerly known as Express Eat, began as a humble idea in Municipality of Daanbantayan-Cebu, providing delicious and accessible meals to students, employees, and local residents. Now reimagined by a team of seven dedicated IT college students, we are combining our passion for technology and love for food to bring you a modern, interactive recipe platform.

Our goal is to make cooking easier and more exciting by offering a diverse collection of recipes from the Philippines and around the world. With our user-friendly search bar, finding your favorite dish or exploring something new has never been more convenient.

As tech enthusiasts, we’ve designed CraveCraft to include step-by-step instructions for every recipe, ensuring that anyone—even beginners—can confidently create delicious meals at home. Behind the scenes, we continue to develop and enhance features to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for our users.

We invite you to join us on this culinary journey as we bridge the gap between technology and cooking. Welcome to CraveCraft, where a group of aspiring IT professionals brings global flavors to your kitchen!
